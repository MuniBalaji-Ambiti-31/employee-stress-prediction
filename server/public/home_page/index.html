About our Project

The impact of stress on employees cannot be overstated, especially in the current global climate. The COVID-19 pandemic has resulted in heightened levels of uncertainty and pressure, leading to increased stress levels for many employees. This stress can manifest in a variety of ways, including decreased job satisfaction, increased absenteeism, and reduced productivity.

To address these issues, organizations must take steps to identify and mitigate the sources of stress in the workplace. Machine learning can be a powerful tool in this regard, as it allows organizations to analyze patterns of employee behavior and identify factors that contribute to stress. By leveraging this data, organizations can develop targeted interventions to reduce stress and improve employee well-being and organizational performance.
